Biography

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons. He is known for transforming Tata Group into a global business conglomerate with operations across multiple industries. Under his leadership, Tata Motors launched the revolutionary Nano car, and Tata Steel acquired Corus, an iconic British steel company. A visionary leader and humanitarian, Tata's commitment to social responsibility has left an indelible mark on Indian society, inspiring millions to strive for excellence and contribute to the nation's progress.
